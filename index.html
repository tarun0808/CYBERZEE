SECURITY

Transaction security refers to the measures taken to protect financial transactions and ensure that sensitive information, such as credit card numbers and personal details, is kept confidential and protected from unauthorized access.Consumers can also take steps to protect themselves, such as regularly monitoring their financial accounts and using strong passwords. Ensuring transaction security is essential for both businesses and consumers to protect their assets and maintain confidence in the financial system.
